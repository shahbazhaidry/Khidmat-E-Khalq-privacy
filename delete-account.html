Delete Account – Khidmat-E-Khalq Community Care Network

This page explains how members of the Khidmat-E-Khalq app can request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to Request Account Deletion

Since Khidmat-E-Khalq is an invite-only community app, account deletion must be requested through the admin. Follow these steps:

Step 1 Send an email to angularcafe@gmail.com
Step 2 Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request – Khidmat-E-Khalq
Step 3 Include your full name and the phone number registered in the app
Step 4 Your account and data will be deleted within 7 business days

What Data Gets Deleted

Data Type Action Retention Period
Name & Phone Number Permanently deleted Deleted within 7 days of request
Google Account (Email) Permanently deleted from Firebase Auth Deleted within 7 days of request
Contribution Records Anonymized or deleted Up to 24 months for financial records
Transaction History Anonymized or deleted Up to 24 months for financial records

Contact

For any questions, email us at angularcafe@gmail.com
